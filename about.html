Our Story

Sole & Story began in a tiny studio with a simple idea: shoes should move with you. What started as limited runs for friends and neighbors grew into a boutique label trusted for fit-first design and durable construction.

We obsess over last shapes, foam densities, and leathers that age gracefully — so you can put your best foot forward, day after day.

Materials & Craft

We source Gold-rated leathers, recycled meshes, and low-VOC adhesives. Our soles blend natural rubber with responsive EVA for balanced cushioning and grip.
